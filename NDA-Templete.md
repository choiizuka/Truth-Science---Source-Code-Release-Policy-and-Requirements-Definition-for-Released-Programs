### Article 1 (Confidential Information)

"Confidential Information" herein means any technical, business, or operational information (including, but not limited to, ideas, system architectures, logics, UI specifications, source code, algorithms, and demo data) disclosed by one party ("Disclosing Party") to the other ("Receiving Party") in connection with the Purpose, whether in writing, orally, in electronic data, or by any other means. Upon termination of the Purpose or upon immediate request by the Disclosing Party, the Receiving Party shall, in accordance with the instructions of the Disclosing Party, promptly return all Confidential Information (including all copies and reproductions thereof) or completely destroy it in an irrecoverable and un-restorable manner.

### Article 5 (Term & Survival)

   1. This Agreement shall become effective on the date of execution and, even after the conclusion of the Purpose, shall remain in effect indefinitely without any time limitation, unless and until the Confidential Information defined in Article 1 fully enters the public domain.
   2. ### Article 6 (Damages and Injunctive Relief)

In the event that the Receiving Party violates any provision of this Agreement, the Disclosing Party shall be entitled to claim full compensation for all direct and indirect damages incurred as a result of such violation, and shall also have the right to seek immediate injunctive relief from a court of competent jurisdiction to restrain or prevent any ongoing or threatened violating act.
